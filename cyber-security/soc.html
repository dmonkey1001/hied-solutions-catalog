Security Operations Center (SOC)

Providing 24/7/365 monitoring and response to protect against cyber threats.

← Back to Cyber Security

A Security Operations Center (SOC) is the centralized command center for an organization's cybersecurity defense. The SOC provides government agencies with advanced technology to continuously monitor, detect, analyze, and respond to cybersecurity incidents. This proactive approach helps to identify and neutralize threats before they can cause significant damage.

Key Features

24/7 Threat Monitoring

Continuous monitoring of networks, servers, and endpoints to detect malicious activity in real-time.

Incident Response & Remediation

Expert-led response to contain threats, eradicate malware, and recover systems following a security incident.

Threat Intelligence

Leveraging global threat intelligence feeds to proactively hunt for and defend against emerging attack techniques.

Compliance Reporting

Regular reporting and documentation to help meet regulatory and compliance requirements like CJIS and HIPAA.
