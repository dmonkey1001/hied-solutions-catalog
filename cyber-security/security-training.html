Security Awareness Training

Building a human firewall to be the first line of defense against cyberattacks.

Even with the best technology, the human element remains a critical factor in cybersecurity. Security Awareness Training solutions are designed to educate and empower government employees to become a strong first line of defense. Through engaging training modules and realistic phishing simulations, we help build a culture of security, reducing the risk of human error that can lead to costly data breaches.

Key Features

Phishing Simulations

Regular, automated phishing tests to train employees to identify and report malicious emails.

Interactive Training Modules

A library of short, engaging videos and training modules covering topics like password security, social engineering, and data handling.

Compliance Training

Specific training modules to help meet compliance requirements for regulations like HIPAA and PCI-DSS.

Reporting & Analytics

Dashboards to track employee training progress and phishing simulation results to identify areas for improvement.
