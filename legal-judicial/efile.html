Electronic Filing (e-Filing)

Providing secure and convenient 24/7 access to the court system.

← Back to Legal & Judicial

Electronic filing transforms the courthouse from a physical location to a 24/7 digital service. Our e-Filing solutions provide secure, web-based portals for attorneys and self-represented litigants to submit documents, pay fees, and receive court notices electronically. This eliminates paper, reduces delays associated with mail and couriers, and makes the justice system more accessible and efficient for everyone.

Key Features

Secure Online Portal

An intuitive, web-based interface for filers to upload documents and manage their case filings from anywhere.

Integrated Fee Payment

Secure processing of filing fees and court costs via credit card or ACH, integrated directly into the filing workflow.

Automated Clerk Review Queue

A digital queue for court clerks to efficiently review, accept, or return filings with automated notifications to the filer.

Direct CMS Integration

Seamless integration with the court's case management system to automatically docket accepted filings and documents.
