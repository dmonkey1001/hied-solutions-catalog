Virtual & Hybrid Courtrooms

Increasing access to justice through secure and reliable remote proceeding technology.

Virtual and hybrid court proceedings have become essential for maintaining court operations and improving access to justice. Our solutions provide secure, high-quality audio and video technology specifically designed for the unique needs of the courtroom. We enable remote appearances for attorneys, defendants, and witnesses, while providing judges with the tools they need to manage the virtual proceeding effectively and maintain the decorum of the court.

Key Features

High-Quality Video & Audio

Professional-grade cameras, microphones, and displays to ensure all participants can be seen and heard clearly.

Secure Virtual Meeting Rooms

Secure platforms with features like virtual waiting rooms, private attorney-client breakout rooms, and judicial controls.

Evidence Presentation

Tools for securely sharing and displaying digital evidence to all participants, both in-person and remote.

Live Streaming & Recording

Capabilities to live stream proceedings for public access and record sessions to create an official court record.
