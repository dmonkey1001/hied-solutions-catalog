Integrated Court Case Management

A single source of truth for all court information, from filing to disposition.

Modern court case management systems (CMS) are the operational heart of the judiciary. These comprehensive platforms provide a centralized database for all case information, including documents, dockets, schedules, and parties. By integrating with other justice partners and automating workflows, a CMS reduces manual data entry, improves data accuracy, and provides judges and court staff with the information they need to manage caseloads effectively.

Key Features

Electronic Document Management

Securely store and manage all case-related documents electronically, with version control and access tracking.

Automated Docketing & Scheduling

Tools to automatically generate dockets, schedule hearings, and manage judicial calendars to avoid conflicts.

Workflow Automation

Configurable workflows to automate routine tasks, such as sending notifications and generating standard court orders.

Integration with Justice Partners

Secure data exchange with prosecutors, public defenders, and law enforcement to create a seamless flow of information.
