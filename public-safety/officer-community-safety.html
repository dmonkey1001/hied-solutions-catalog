Officer & Community Safety

Technology that protects first responders and builds public trust.

The safety of law enforcement officers and the communities they serve is paramount. We provide technologies that enhance situational awareness, promote de-escalation, and increase transparency in police-citizen interactions. These tools are designed not only to protect officers in the line of duty but also to foster accountability and strengthen the relationship between law enforcement and the public.

Example Solutions

Body-Worn & In-Car Cameras

Integrated video solutions that provide a clear record of events, with automated activation and secure cloud storage.

De-escalation Technology

Less-lethal devices and tools that provide officers with more options to safely resolve volatile situations.

Officer Wellness Applications

Mobile apps and resources to support the mental and physical health of first responders.

Situational Awareness Platforms

Tools that provide officers with real-time information about a call for service before they arrive on scene.
