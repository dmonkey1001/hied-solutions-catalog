Crime Prevention & Predictive Policing

Using data to proactively deploy resources and deter criminal activity.

← Back to Public Safety & Cyber Security

Modern policing is shifting from a reactive to a proactive model. Our crime prevention and predictive policing solutions leverage historical data and advanced analytics to identify patterns and forecast areas with a high risk of criminal activity. This allows law enforcement agencies to strategically deploy resources, engage with the community, and deter crime before it happens, creating safer neighborhoods for everyone.

Example Solutions

Crime Analysis & Hotspot Mapping

GIS-based tools that analyze crime data to visualize hotspots, patterns, and trends for command staff and officers.

Predictive Policing Software

Algorithms that forecast the times and locations where specific types of crime are most likely to occur, guiding patrol assignments.

Community Engagement Platforms

Tools that facilitate communication between police and residents, such as online tip submission forms and neighborhood watch portals.

Real-Time Crime Centers

Technology to equip command centers with live video feeds, gunshot detection alerts, and data dashboards for situational awareness.
