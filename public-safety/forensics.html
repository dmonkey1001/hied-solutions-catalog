Equipping crime labs with the technology to uncover the truth from evidence.

Forensic science plays a pivotal role in the justice system. As crime becomes more technologically sophisticated, crime labs need advanced tools to process evidence accurately and efficiently. We provide a range of digital and physical forensic solutions that help examiners extract, analyze, and report on evidence from electronic devices, video footage, and crime scenes, ensuring scientific rigor and admissibility in court.

Example Solutions

Digital Forensics Tools

Hardware and software for extracting and analyzing data from computers, mobile phones, and other digital devices.

Video & Audio Analysis

Software to clarify, enhance, and analyze video and audio recordings from surveillance systems and other sources.

Laboratory Information Management (LIMS)

Systems to manage lab workflows, track evidence, record test results, and maintain a secure chain of custody.

Crime Scene Documentation

3D laser scanners and software to accurately capture and diagram crime scenes for investigation and courtroom presentation.
