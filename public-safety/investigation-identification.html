Investigation & Identification

Providing law enforcement with advanced tools to solve crimes faster.

Effective criminal investigations rely on the ability to quickly gather, process, and analyze evidence to identify suspects. We provide a suite of advanced investigative and identification technologies that empower detectives and analysts. From biometric systems to digital evidence management platforms, these tools help accelerate case resolution, improve accuracy, and ensure a secure chain of custody for all evidence.

Example Solutions

Biometric Identification Systems

Automated Fingerprint Identification Systems (AFIS) and facial recognition technology to compare evidence against databases.

Digital Evidence Management (DEMS)

Centralized, cloud-based platforms to securely store, manage, and share digital evidence from body-cams, cell phones, and more.

Link Analysis Software

Tools that visualize relationships between people, places, and events from various data sources to uncover hidden connections.

License Plate Recognition (LPR)

Systems that automatically capture and analyze license plate data to identify vehicles of interest in real-time.
