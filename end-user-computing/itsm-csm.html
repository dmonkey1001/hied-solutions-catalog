Customer and Service Management (ITSM / CSM)

Streamlining service delivery and support for internal and external customers.

← Back to End User Computing & Support

Excellent service delivery is critical for both internal government employees and the citizens they serve. IT Service Management (ITSM) and Customer Service Management (CSM) platforms provide a structured approach to managing service requests, incidents, and problems. By implementing best-practice workflows, these solutions help IT help desks and other service departments improve responsiveness, resolve issues faster, and increase customer satisfaction.

Example Solutions

IT Help Desk & Ticketing

Centralized platforms for logging, tracking, and resolving IT support tickets, with automated routing and escalation.

Self-Service Portals & Knowledge Base

User-friendly portals where employees can find answers to common IT questions, reset passwords, and submit service requests.

Change & Release Management

Formal processes and tools to manage changes to IT systems, minimizing service disruptions and risk.

Enterprise Service Management

Extending ITSM principles to other departments like HR and Facilities to manage their own service requests and workflows.
