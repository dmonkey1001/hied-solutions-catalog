Cyber Security

Protecting critical infrastructure, sensitive data, and public trust from evolving digital threats.

← Back to Solutions Catalog

In an era of increasing digital threats, a robust cybersecurity posture is non-negotiable for state and local governments. Solutions should aim to provide a multi-layered defense to protect sensitive citizen data and ensure the continuity of critical public services. Comprehensive strategies and solutions should address everything from the network perimeter to the end user, ensuring the integrity, confidentiality, and availability of government systems.

Featured Solutions

Security Operations Center (SOC)

Managed security services providing 24/7 threat monitoring, detection, and incident response to protect against cyberattacks.

Identity & Access Management (IAM)

Solutions to ensure that only authorized individuals have access to sensitive systems and data, featuring multi-factor authentication.

Endpoint Detection & Response (EDR)

Advanced protection for desktops, laptops, and servers that detects and responds to sophisticated threats like ransomware.

Security Awareness Training

Training programs and phishing simulations to empower employees to recognize and report potential security threats.
