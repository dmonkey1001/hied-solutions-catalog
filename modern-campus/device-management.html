Device Management & Inventory

Managing the entire lifecycle of your technology assets securely and efficiently.

← Back to End User Computing & Support

Managing thousands of devices across a government enterprise is a complex challenge. Device management and inventory solutions provide a centralized platform to automate deployment, enforce security policies, distribute software, and track assets from procurement to disposal. This ensures all devices are secure, compliant, and properly accounted for, reducing risk and operational overhead.

Example Solutions

Unified Endpoint Management (UEM)

A single console to manage and secure desktops, laptops, tablets, and smartphones (iOS & Android) across the organization.

Zero-Touch Deployment

Services like Windows Autopilot and Apple Business Manager to automatically enroll and configure new devices without IT intervention.

IT Asset Management (ITAM)

Systems to track hardware and software inventory, manage licenses, and monitor asset lifecycle to optimize spending.

Patch Management & Software Distribution

Automated tools to deploy security patches and software updates to all endpoints, ensuring systems are protected from vulnerabilities.
