Geographic Information Systems (GIS)

Visualizing data to reveal location-based patterns, trends, and insights.

← Back to Data & Analytics

Location is a critical component of nearly all government data. Geographic Information Systems (GIS) provide the tools to map, analyze, and interpret data in a geographic context. From planning and zoning to public safety and infrastructure management, our GIS solutions help agencies visualize complex information, optimize service delivery, and make more informed, spatially-aware decisions.

Example Solutions

Public-Facing Web Maps

Interactive online maps for citizens to find information about zoning, capital projects, parks, and election polling places.

Infrastructure & Asset Mapping

Mapping and managing the location and condition of public assets like water pipes, roads, and traffic signals.

Public Safety Analysis

Crime mapping and analysis tools to identify hotspots, as well as platforms for emergency response and routing.

Mobile Field Data Collection

GIS-enabled mobile apps for field crews to collect and update data on asset conditions, inspections, and service calls.
