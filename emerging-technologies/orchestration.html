Orchestration

Automating complex IT processes to improve speed, reliability, and efficiency.

← Back to Emerging Technologies

IT orchestration is the automated configuration, coordination, and management of complex computer systems and software. Instead of manually performing sequences of tasks, orchestration tools connect disparate systems and automate entire workflows. This leads to faster service delivery, reduced human error, improved security, and allows IT staff to focus on more strategic initiatives.

Example Solutions

Automated Server Provisioning

Workflows that automatically deploy and configure virtual servers in the cloud or on-premise data centers in minutes, not days.

Security Orchestration (SOAR)

Automating incident response playbooks by integrating security tools to instantly quarantine threats and notify personnel.

Disaster Recovery Automation

Automated failover of critical systems to a secondary site during an outage, ensuring business continuity.

Employee Onboarding/Offboarding

A single workflow that creates or revokes user accounts and access rights across dozens of systems simultaneously.
