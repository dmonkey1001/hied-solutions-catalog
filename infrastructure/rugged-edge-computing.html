Edge computing brings data processing and storage closer to the source of data generation, enabling faster insights and real-time responses. For government, this is critical for in-field operations, public safety, and IoT initiatives. Our rugged and edge computing solutions provide the durable hardware and localized processing power needed to operate effectively in mobile environments and remote locations, even with limited connectivity.

Example Solutions

In-Vehicle Computing

Rugged computers, routers, and gateways designed for patrol cars, fire trucks, and public works vehicles.

IoT Gateways

Devices that aggregate and process data from smart city sensors locally before sending necessary information to the cloud.

Mobile Data Centers

Containerized data centers that can be rapidly deployed to provide computing capabilities for emergency operations centers.

Video Analytics at the Edge

Edge devices that can analyze video streams from traffic or security cameras in real-time to identify incidents.
