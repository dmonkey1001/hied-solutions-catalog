Infrastructure Modernization

Replacing aging infrastructure with agile, efficient, and secure modern platforms.

← Back to Infrastructure

Aging IT infrastructure can be a significant barrier to innovation, posing security risks and incurring high maintenance costs. Our infrastructure modernization services help governments strategically upgrade their core systems. We focus on replacing outdated servers, storage, and networking equipment with modern, software-defined solutions that are more agile, automated, and aligned with cloud-native principles.

Example Solutions

Data Center Consolidation

Services to consolidate multiple aging data centers into a smaller, more efficient, and modern footprint.

Hyperconverged Infrastructure (HCI)

Integrated systems that combine compute, storage, and networking into a single, software-defined platform for simplified management.

Mainframe Modernization

Strategies to migrate applications off legacy mainframes to more flexible and cost-effective cloud or on-premise platforms.

Software-Defined Networking (SDN)

Modernizing the network with centralized, software-based control for increased agility and automation.
